**Editorial notes:**
- The italic in the home card renders the question as written. Don't append a question mark if the seed copy doesn't already have one.
- Keep questions under 18 words. The two outliers above are intentional and tested — anything longer crowds the hero.
- Categories are for filtering on the archive page. Don't expose them in the prompt UI itself.
- We will rotate in fresh prompts roughly quarterly. Treat this seed as immutable so old `Memory.promptId` references don't break.
